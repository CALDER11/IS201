Climates

Mexico is within the top 15 most climatically diverse countries in the world. With varying topography, and a wide range of territoy you can find almost everything in Mexico. Tall cold mountains, extreme desserts, pinewood forests, grasslands, rainforest, lakes, rivers, and thousands of miles of pristeen coastline. This also allows for a wide variety of produce to be cultivated which contributes to its exquisite cousine.

Food Destinations

If your reason for visiting Mexico is to eat, let me give you some pointers. The finest, and most famous dishes in Mexico are found in Puebla. Known for the famous mole poblano, and many other iconic dishes, it is a must for anyone who is a foodie. But, if you prefer flour tortillas over corn, and love barbeque, I would direct you towards the north to Sonora. This state is know for its cattle ranches and fat burritos. the best carne asada is found in Sonora. it won't disappoint. Other honorable mentions include Mexico City, Oaxaca, and Merida. These cities each have their own unique flair that adds interest to their cousine.
